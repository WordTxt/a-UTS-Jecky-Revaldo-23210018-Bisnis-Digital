Digital Business program

The Digital Business study program prepares graduates who have superior digipreneur skills and are able to develop and apply qualified digital technology. Students will be equipped with theory-based learning, practice, and modern lecture facilities so that students can get an optimal learning experience. The Digital Business study program has three main concentrations, namely Digital Marketing, Technopreneur, and Digital Business Analyst.

Profile of Graduates of the Digital Business Study Program

  • A superior work ethic with a Christian worldview as the basis for running his life, both personal and professional.

  • Neat business management concepts. Graduates of this study program will master business management concepts neatly and be able to translate these concepts into appropriate business actions.

  • Konsep berpikir kreatif dan Higher Order Thinking Skills (HOTS) yang tertampilkan dalam berbagai rencana bisnis dan pemanfaatan digital tools dalam mendukung dan menyukseskan rencana bisnis.

  • Digital entrepreneurial skills, including good and effective communication skills, managing information wisely, innovative digital content creation, data-driven problem solving, and deep knowledge of business security in the digital era.

  • Global perspective. Graduates of this study program will quickly respond and carefully analyze global issues related to digital business and how they will utilize global information to create advantages for their business products.
