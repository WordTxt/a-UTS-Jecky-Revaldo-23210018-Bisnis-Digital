Information Systems & Technology Program

The Information Systems and Technology (STI) study program is a combination of Information Systems (IS) and Information Technology (IT) to answer the challenges of rapid technological development and digital transformation. STI studies how to design and implement digital products (applications, websites, and designs) that meet business objectives and solve everyday life problems. STI also studies emerging technologies.

Profile of Graduates of the Information Systems and Technology Study Program

  • Graduates are able to act and judge professionally.

  • Graduates are able to think logically, critically and systematically in utilizing informatics/computer science knowledge to solve real problems.

  • Graduates have the ability to analyze, design, create, and evaluate information systems that are aligned with organizational goals.

  • Graduates are able to develop multi-platform based technology products.

  • Graduates are able to apply knowledge in the Programming and Software Development function area to their profession.
